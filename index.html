Welcome to the Quality of Life Dashboard

The 2012 Neighborhood Quality of Life Study helps officials and residents measure the health of Charlotte and Mecklenburg County neighborhoods. The study divides the County into 464 Neighborhood Profile Areas (NPA) to examine quality of life variables. Features of the 2012 study include:

  • Inclusion of towns within Mecklenburg County
  • Collaboration with Mecklenburg County GIS
  • Expansion of data variables from 20 to 80
  • New metric categories - Character, Engagement, Economics, Health, Safety, Education, Environment and Housing
  • Stable, Transitioning and Challenged labels are no longer used
  • This web portal has been created to allow easy access to the data

The secrect to successful searches is to let the search box do the work for you. Select a match using either the mouse or the up/down arrow keys and Enter.

Address: Enter the first part of the address. Ex: for 700 North Tryon Street, try 700 n

Place: Enter any part of a place name. Ex: type carolina

Street: Enter the name of the street. Ex: type ruth

Intersection: Enter a street name, followed by an &. Ex: type ruth &

Parcel ID: Enter an 8-digit Parcel ID. Ex: type 11111111

Your Current Location: If your web browser supports geolocation, you will be prompted to allow its use and you will see Find My Location under the search bar.
